What is Disney+?

What can I watch on Disney+?
Disney+ offers you a constantly growing collection of stories:
  • Exclusive original series and movies
  • Timeless classics and recent hits produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Even more entertainment with comedies, dramas, true-story-inspired content, and much more

    • What is the price of a Disney+ subscription?
    You can choose from three Disney+ subscription plans:
  • Premium (€11.99/month or €119.90/year): Up to 4K Ultra HD and HDR resolution. 4 simultaneous streams. Downloads on up to 10 devices. Dolby Atmos audio on supported content. Ad-free.
  • Standard (€8.99/month or €89.90/year): Up to 1080p Full HD resolution. 2 simultaneous streams. Downloads on up to 10 devices. 5.1 surround sound on supported content. Ad-free.
  • Standard with ads (€5.99/month): Up to 1080p Full HD resolution. 2 simultaneous streams. 5.1 surround sound on supported content.
    At the end of your subscription period, your subscription will automatically renew for the same duration at the Premium rate in effect at that time (currently those listed above), unless you cancel beforehand or select another plan.
    You can also gift a one-year Disney+ subscription to a friend or family member by clicking here.
    All subscription plans may include trailers for our theatrical releases, promotion of Disney products and services, sponsorship messages, and other limited promotional content. Audio and video quality depend on your internet connection and device capabilities. Standard with ads: not available on certain devices and does not allow downloads.

    Does the Disney+ subscription include a commitment period? Disney+ has no commitment, and you can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the billing period. To unsubscribe, follow these 6 steps:
